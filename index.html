Cuyahoga Valley Code Shop Home

A home workplace away from your workplace.

Traditional 9-to-5 jobs are not for everybody. I learned that lesson firsthand after jumping from position to position, wondering why I couldn't commit to any for longer than 18 months.

After taking the leap and deciding to go off on my own, I quickly found out that there was a world of things I was missing by renting out my brain for someone else's agenda.

I got to pursue on my own projects, define my day, and work from home. The dream had been realized! Until, no longer was I inspired to put on pants for the day. Nor share the things I was learning with other people. I decided it was time to try the co-working craze.

Except, that didn't work either. I was surrounded by freelancers of all kinds - copywriters, marketers, 3D animators. People who were extremely talented in what they were pursuing, but our common ground was that we both liked tacos.

I also noticed that by attending a co-working space, I was giving up any opportunity to actually get things done. It seemed that I excelled at becoming more social, but to the point where it had a negative effect on my productivity.

Welcome to the Code Shop

The Cuyahoga Valley Code Shop is a space where we strive to strike a balance between the collaboration that occurs through socialization while remaining productive. In other words: we want to help you get shit done by being around other people who care about their craft.

What's included with the space?

Private Patio + BBQ
Bring your laptop out on the patio and bring some grub to toss on the grill.
Security
Leave your belongings or take them with you. We have a lockitron system so we know who is in and who is out at all times.

Why should I come?

Accountability
It's easy to work from home, but it isn't always easy to hold yourself accountable. Be around other people who will help make sure you get shit done.
Collaboration
Meet other developers, give and receive help with others working through problems, and become a part of a community.
No bullshit
We're not here to accelerate your startup or pitch you an investment opportunity. There are plenty of other spaces for that. If you want to build things with like-minded folks, this is the place for you.
Self-Improvement
Learn about starting your own business, productizing, code katas, be the worst in the room. Be around other smarties.
Work Referrals
Get project/client leads
Community
Monthly Meetups in cellar/patio

What makes you any different?

In the National Park
Peninsula is the only city located inside of the National Park. It's awesome and feels like a vacation town. Get out of the city and get outside.
Delicious Provisions
We're located steps away from an eclectic candy shop and the Winking Lizard, a staple in Northeast Ohio known for their variety of beers. Got vices? Want them?
Code Campfire
Once a month, we host a campfire cookout where you can come and check out the space, talk shop, or just hang out and meet people.
Bicycle Storage
Bring your bike once and keep it down here. When the weather gets nice, you can take it for a spin whenever you want on the best roads and trails in Northeast Ohio.
