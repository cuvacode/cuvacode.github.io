Are there membership fees?

Money is not the objective.

Once a month, you should factor in contributing one or more of the following:

  • Write an article
  • Record a video tutorial
  • Prep a talk
  • Lead a workshop/class
  • Submit pull requests to an organization project
  • 10 hours of pair programming availability
  • Supply beer and coffee

If none of these work for you, you can alternatively contribute $150 a month as a membership fee.
